Roof Systems

Inteva's heritage of experience in roof systems dates back to the Golde company, one of the pioneers in roof systems. Today we are applying the latest technologies to meet leading automakers' needs for roofs in all shapes, sizes and configurations, while sharing our advanced development concepts and vision for the future of the sunroof in electric and autonomous vehicles.

Inteva's 20-plus roof sites around the world are dedicated to minimizing noise and mass while maximizing opening size, body strength, durability and styling flexibility.

From award-winning panoramic roofs to high-value one-step innovations and tilt-and-slide technologies, Inteva's InScape portfolio is unmatched. Our vertical integration provides options such as integrated wind deflectors, sunshades, roller blind systems and motors.

  • Sliding Technology

    There's nothing quite like the feel of fresh air to connect you to the surrounding landscape. Inteva's sliding technology options make it that much easier for automakers to provide the best solution to fit their needs.

  • Panoramic Offerings

    Escape from the inside with Inteva's panoramic roofs. Our panoramic sunroofs are some of the largest in the industry that also meet customer demands for low weight and structural integrity.

  • All InScape™ Offerings

    When it comes to providing sunroof solutions, the sky’s the limit. Inteva's wide portfolio of options provides the latest and greatest in sunroof technology.
